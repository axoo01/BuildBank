Build Smarter,Track Better!

A simple, powerful construction expense tracker designed for small teams and property owners, staying in control of your project’s budget from the first brick to the final receipt.

BuildBank is your simple, secure, and intuitive partner for tracking construction expenses. Whether you're a small team, a family-run project, or just managing your own build, BuildBank helps you take control of your budget with zero guesswork.

We believe that financial clarity should be effortless. That’s why we’ve built a tool that focuses on ease of use, transparent tracking, and practical features without overwhelming you with complexity. With BuildBank, you focus on the building. We’ll handle the numbers.
